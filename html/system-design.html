How PICKS Is Organized

Main system

PICKS

Helps users see inventory, connect related records, support lab work, check access, and share information with other systems.

Design Intent

Keep PICKS flexible so GTRI can change ERP, MES, or older data connections without replacing everything.

Primary Information Path

A request is checked, matched with purchasing and funding information, connected to lab history, used for inventory or lab work, and recorded.

What Matters Most

Security, connected history, reliability, speed, and flexibility drive how PICKS is organized.
